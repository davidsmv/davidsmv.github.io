About Me

Hello there! My name is David Martinez and I am a business administrator with a focus on finance and internationalization processes.

I have experience in purchasing, consultancy, and pricing data analysis. I consider myself an organized, creative, and committed person who is always eager to learn new things. I strongly believe that technology is the key tool for the progress of our society.

Skills: SQL, Power BI, Tableau, Holistics, Web Scraping, and Python. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge in Machine Learning.

Experience:
- Business Intelligence Analyst at Tul (Colombian startup): Handled ETL processes and market information tracking for Colombia, Brazil & Mexico.
- Pricing Analyst at Habi (Proptech): Responsible for real estate pricing models and data analysis.

If you'd like to connect, feel free to reach out via LinkedIn.
